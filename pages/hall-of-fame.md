The **Hall of Fame** page celebrates people, moments, and contributions that have shaped **Codershigh**.

This space is meant to grow into a living record of:

- memorable learner journeys
- standout contributions in sessions
- projects and ideas that carried the spirit of Codershigh forward
- people who helped build, sustain, and deepen the community
- milestones worth remembering

For now, this page is the starting point. As the project evolves, it can hold profiles of learners, builders, contributors, and memorable moments.

Codershigh is not just a collection of sessions. It is a community shaped by people who think carefully, contribute generously, and help others grow. The Hall of Fame is meant to remember that.
