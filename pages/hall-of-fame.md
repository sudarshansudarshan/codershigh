The **Hall of Fame** page celebrates people, moments, and contributions that have shaped **Codershigh**.

This space is meant to grow into a living record of:

- memorable learner journeys
- standout contributions in sessions
- projects and ideas that carried the spirit of Codershigh forward
- people who helped build, sustain, and deepen the community
- milestones worth remembering

For now, this page is the starting point.

As the project evolves, it can hold:

## Learners
Profiles of learners whose work, persistence, curiosity, or originality left a mark.

## Builders and Contributors
People who helped create sessions, shape ideas, strengthen the platform, document conversations, or support the wider Codershigh ecosystem.

## Memorable Moments
Selected moments, breakthroughs, reflections, and experiments that capture what makes this space meaningful.

## Why this page exists
Codershigh is not just a collection of sessions. It is a community shaped by people who think carefully, contribute generously, and help others grow.

The Hall of Fame is meant to remember that.
