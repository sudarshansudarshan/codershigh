## What is Codershigh?

Codershigh is a discussion-first learning community for algorithms, computer science, and mathematical thinking. It is built around curiosity, conceptual clarity, and shared intellectual exploration.

## Is it a coding club or a placement group?

No. Codershigh is not built around placement theatre, résumé signalling, or speed-solving culture. It is much closer to a thinking community than a coaching system.

## How do sessions work?

A session usually begins with a question, puzzle, or case study. The group often spends a long time understanding the question itself before moving toward any solution. Discussion is the main method.

## Who is Codershigh for?

It is for learners who enjoy asking “why?” and “what if?”, who are comfortable not knowing, and who value understanding over quick performance.

## Is it math-heavy?

Not in the usual sense. Codershigh values intuition, clarity, and conceptual structure more than intimidating formalism.

## What kinds of topics do you explore?

Algorithms, computer science ideas, problem-solving habits, and concept-rich case studies that open into broader ways of thinking.

## How should a new person begin?

The best starting point is [Season 01](/codershigh/season-01/). After that, the [Handbook](/codershigh/handbook/) helps make the culture and materials more concrete.

## Where do announcements happen?

Announcements are typically shared through the community channels, including WhatsApp and Discord.
