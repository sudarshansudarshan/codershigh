Frequently Asked Questions

What is Codershigh?

A slow-thinking learning community for algorithms, computer science, and mathematical thinking. We explore ideas through discussion, not through solving problems quickly.

How does a session work?

A session usually begins with a question, puzzle, or case study. The group stays with it — sometimes for hours — unpacking what it really means, what assumptions hide inside it, and what deeper ideas connect to it.

Who is it for?

Anyone who wants to understand ideas deeply and is comfortable with not finishing quickly, with not having all the answers, and with thinking out loud together.

Is this for placement preparation?

No. Codershigh is explicitly not about placement theatre, résumé building, or competitive problem-solving. The goal is clarity, not quickness.

Do I need to be an expert?

No. What matters is curiosity and willingness to engage, not existing expertise.
