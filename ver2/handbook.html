Handbook

This handbook reframes Codershigh on its own terms, instead of borrowing language from other initiatives.

Session culture

  • Questions first
  • No rush to closure
  • Clarity over jargon
  • Collaborative reasoning over performance

Participation norms

  • Naïve questions are welcome
  • Disagreement should clarify, not dominate
  • Slowness is not a weakness here
  • The group moves at the speed of understanding

What participants gain

Better intuition, confidence in discussing difficult ideas, comfort with uncertainty, and a more durable relationship with problem solving.

What facilitators do

Facilitators do not simply reveal answers. They hold the space, connect ideas, sharpen the question, and help the group notice deeper structure.
