Handbook

session culture, participation norms, and facilitator role

Session culture

  • questions first
  • no rush to closure
  • clarity over jargon
  • collective reasoning over performance

Participation norms

  • naïve questions are welcome
  • disagreement should clarify, not dominate
  • slowness is a strength here

What facilitators do

Facilitators hold the space, connect ideas, sharpen the question, and help the group notice deeper structure.
