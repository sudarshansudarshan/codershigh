faq

what is codershigh?

A slow-thinking learning community for algorithms, computer science, and mathematical thinking.

how does a session work?

A session begins with a question, puzzle, or case study. The group stays with it long enough to understand what the problem is really asking and what deeper ideas are hiding inside it.

who is it for?

Anyone who values curiosity over speed, and understanding over polished performance.

is this for placement preparation?

No. Codershigh is explicitly not built around placement theatre or competitive speed-solving.
