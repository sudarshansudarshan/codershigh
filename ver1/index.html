Codershigh is a discussion-first learning community for algorithms, computer science, and deep intellectual adventure.

A cleaner, more academic presentation inspired by the al-folio style.

Codershigh is for people who want to stay with a question long enough to actually understand it. We explore algorithms, mathematical thinking, and computer science through case studies, discomfort, intuition, and collaborative reasoning. This is not coaching. It is not placement theatre. It is not speed-solving culture.

what a session feels like

The Greedy Lawyer

A revenue-maximization puzzle becomes a discussion about assumptions, framing, and why obvious answers are often shallow.

The Book Vendor in Modern Malgudi

A story problem opens into a conversation about optimization, constraints, and the mismatch between textbook and real-world reasoning.

The Placement Conundrum

Instead of solving placement problems, the group questions why we approach such problems the way we do in the first place.

why codershigh feels different

We stay with the question. We think together. We do not worship speed.
  • Question-first: understanding the problem comes before solving it.
  • Discussion-led: participants reason out loud and build understanding together.
  • Depth over performance: the goal is clarity and intuition, not showing off quickness.
